Ribadyms

Ribadym, also known as the ocean’s phantoms or water dogs (courtesy of humans) some says they were victims of the sea, others speculate that they are born from vengeful spirits. Whatever these creatures are, it is agreed upon by any humans that none of them would want to become one. Since being reborn as a Ribadym means being bound to the sea. Life as a ribadym can be a lonely one; the sea is large, deep and vast. When ribadyms meet, they often form a pack. Ribadyms are a social species that usually group together and keep each other company. It's speculated that this is due to human nature to crave companionship but others think that it's just their nature to do so.

Sertyrs

In a world full of otherworldly denizens, there exists a race of faefolk called Sertyr. They stand on two hind legs similar to a deer’s, fragrant flowers growing on their body and have serpentine characteristics. Mischievous and neither malevolent or benevolent, these tricksters leave behind a trail of chaos in their wake. Either through fae magic or through the mysterious natural concoctions their body produces. However, if you win the trust of a Setyr...or amuse them enough to gain favour, Sertyrs will be willing to share their poison and/or antidote which they carry on their glass like horns.
